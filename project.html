What the games is?

Game is a mix of idle clicker and tower defence game. We have used a merge mechanic to upgrade defeneces towers and walls. Our aim was to create nice idle game with strategy aspect and some kind of war with hordes of enemies.

Install now!

We have started the project as students who want to create a nice and advanced project on our faculty and now we are junior developers in Pixel Storm. Now game are released and still developed to create new features, places and gameplay mechanics.
